# Responsible AI Transparency Document

## Project: ElectroBot AI

### 1. Safety Guidelines
ElectroBot AI is built on the foundation of safety and neutrality. We utilize Google Gemini's native safety settings to block:
- **Hate Speech:** All content promoting violence or hatred against protected groups is blocked at the source.
- **Harassment:** Any attempts to use the bot for bullying or harassment are mitigated.
- **Dangerous Content:** Instructions for illegal acts or dangerous activities are strictly prohibited.

### 2. Fairness & Bias Mitigation
In the context of Indian elections, neutrality is key. ElectroBot AI is instructed to:
- **Remain Non-Partisan:** The bot does not promote or criticize any specific political party or candidate.
- **Fact-Based Only:** Responses are limited to official ECI guidelines and constitutional procedures.
- **Balanced Perspectives:** When discussing sensitive topics like NOTA or Election Laws, the bot provides objective, balanced information.

### 3. Human-Centric Design
- **Verification:** Users are encouraged to verify critical registration dates on the official ECI portal.
- **Accessibility:** The bot supports multiple languages to ensure inclusivity for all Indian citizens.

### 4. Technical Guardrails
- **Input Sanitization:** All user prompts are sanitized before being sent to the AI model.
- **Rate Limiting:** Protects the service from abuse and ensures fair access for all users.
