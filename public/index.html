⚖️ Your Voting Rights

Know your fundamental rights as an Indian voter

🔒

Secret Ballot

Your vote is completely secret. No one can force you to reveal who you voted for. This is protected by law.

🚫

Right to NOTA

"None of the Above" allows you to reject all candidates if you find none worthy of your vote.

🛡️

Free from Coercion

No one can threaten, bribe, or force you to vote for a particular party or candidate. Report to 1950.

📋

Right to Information

You have the right to know your candidates' criminal records, assets, and liabilities before voting.

♿

Accessible Voting

All polling booths must be accessible for differently-abled voters. Wheelchair access and Braille EVMs available.

⏰

Paid Leave to Vote

Your employer must give you paid leave on election day. Legal requirement under Representation of People Act.
